Hi, I'm Ross

I’m a TEFL-certified native English teacher from the UK. I have taught hundreds of students and helped them to improve their English level. I love meeting and talking with people from around the world and helping them learn English. I like to talk about travel, music, literature, society, and sports amongst other things.

I prepare each lesson in advance to fit your needs and try to ensure that learning with me will be interesting and informative.

Whether you are preparing for an exam like IELTS, TOEFL, or Cambridge, or you need to improve your English for work or simply as a hobby, I can help you reach your goals. Book a trial class and we can discuss how to work together to get the results you want.
